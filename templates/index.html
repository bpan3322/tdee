TDEE Calculator

The purpose of the TDEE Calculator is to calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure. A measure of how many calories you burn per day.

So how does this work/is this information helpful?

TDEE calculates how many calories (estimate) you burn per day, taking in your exercise levels into account. By knowing your age, weight and height, we can calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is the amount of calories you need everyday to just survive. Then we multiply your BMR by your activity levels. Each activity level is associated to a specific factor, and by multiplying your BMR to your specific activity level factor, we can calculate your Total Daily Expenditure!

So why is this information useful? Well, for starters, if you're new in the fitness industry, or if you're just trying to slim down/bulk up, knowing your calories is very important. If you aim to slim down, then take your TDEE calorie count, and make sure to go under. If you aim to gain weight, then do the opposite!
